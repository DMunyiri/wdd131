Kenya's Landscape

Kenya, often referred to as the "Cradle of Humanity" due to its rich archaeological history, is a country of breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cultures, and remarkable wildlife. Located in East Africa, it is renowned for its stunning natural reserves like the Maasai Mara, Amboseli, and Tsavo, which are home to the "Big Five" and host the iconic Great Migration. Beyond its natural beauty, Kenya has emerged as a hub for innovation, particularly in mobile technology, with the advent of M-Pesa transforming financial transactions across Africa.
